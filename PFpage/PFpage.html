What’s the first thing that pops into your head when you hear the word ‘animation’? Cartoons, of course! Cartoons and indeed animation have come a long way since the creation of the first iconic characters like Betty Boop, Popeye the Sailor and Daffy Duck during the 1930’s. Nowadays we have companies like Disney, Pixar and DreamWorks, producing full length animated movies which have become classics and loved by viewers of all ages.
